About us

Bedford Falls - Georgian rock band. Formed in 2014. It includes: Dato Jikia (vocals , rhythm guitar), Mikheil Kalandarishvili (lead guitar), Giorgi Migriauli (bass guitar) and Sandro Diasamidze (drums) by 2020 . Their music includes songs from the indie-rock genre, most of which are in English. Among the band's most famous songs are "Thrill is Gone", "Dreamer Once", "I Wonder". The band's name, Bedford Falls, is associated with the town of the same name in the famous 1946 film "This Is a Wonderful Life". In the summer of 2016 , 2017 and 2019 , Bedford Falls played at the Tbilisi Open Air .
